Craig's CoffeeHouse

About Craig's

Founded in 1996, Craig's Coffeehouse travels the world in pursuit of coffees with the highest quality, complexity, and balance. We take a hands-on approach to coffee. Dedicating ourselves to learning about soil conditions and sustainable farming practices are only a few of the areas that have helped us source outstanding coffees.

At Craig's core is the desire to share the delicious coffees that we are excited about. From our shop's dedicated regulars to coffee lovers around the globe, we enjoy coffee together.
